## Design Context

### Users
Founders and CTOs evaluating senior engineering partners for AI-driven products. They arrive skeptical (burned by agencies before), time-constrained, and looking for signals of real expertise. The site must convert high-intent visitors into Calendly bookings. Secondary audience: LATAM Spanish-speaking founders.

### Brand Personality
**Bold, Direct, Modern** — No hedging, no filler. The site speaks with the confidence of senior engineers who've shipped. Every element earns its place.

### Emotional Goal
**Confidence & calm** — "These people clearly know what they're doing — I can relax." The interface should feel inevitable, not effortful. Visitors should sense mastery in the restraint.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Premium product feel with rich, purposeful interactions — inspired by Stripe and Raycast
- **Typography**: Editorial contrast (Playfair Display serif for headings, Inter sans for body) — prestige meets clarity
- **Color**: Warm paper surface (#F5F7FA) with ink text (#111), purple-to-pink gradients for energy and accent
- **Motion**: Subtle but sophisticated — 3D parallax, smooth hover lifts, shimmer gradients. Always respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- **Anti-references**: Generic agency sites, stock photo layouts, buzzword-heavy copy, overly playful/startup-y aesthetics

### Design Principles
1. **Earned presence** — Every element must justify its existence. No decorative filler.
2. **Quiet confidence** — Premium is felt in spacing, typography, and restraint — not in flash.
3. **Interaction as proof** — Micro-interactions and polish signal engineering quality without saying it.
4. **Clarity over cleverness** — Copy and UI should be immediately understood. No jargon gates.
5. **Accessible by default** — Current a11y patterns (focus-visible, reduced-motion, semantic HTML) are the floor, not the ceiling.

### Technical Foundations
- **Stack**: Next.js 14 (App Router), React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS 3
- **Fonts**: Inter (--font-inter), Playfair Display (--font-playfair) via next/font
- **Colors**: surface (#F5F7FA), ink (#111), purple-600/blue-600 gradient accents, pink (#ff9a9e, #fbc2eb) highlights
- **Spacing**: Generous — py-24 sections, p-8 cards, max-w-5xl/6xl containers
- **Effects**: Glassmorphism (backdrop-blur, white/70 bg), hover lift (translate-y), gradient dividers
- **i18n**: English + Spanish with Accept-Language detection
